Ensemble

source_code

Ensemble is a fashion app that allows users to get feedback on what they are wearing, while allowing them to see what others wore to the same kind of events. This responsive web app was built in Rails, JavaScript, and Handlebars. API's implemented: Instagram and Cloudinary.

Bugzify

source_code

With the Bugzify Mobile App children earn points that have real monetary value in exchange for completing tasks assigned by their parents. Bugzify is not just for chores, it helps the completion of any request, from a child requesting his mom to pick him up after school or asking a friend for a favor. This Android mobile app is being built in AngularJS and ionic.
